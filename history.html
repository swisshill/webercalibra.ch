Our History

Precision since 1924

A family business

In 1924, Walter Weber founded Weber + Cie in the small village of La Heutte, Switzerland. Fifty years later, in 1974, Walter's sons Willy and Hugo Weber founded Calibra SA, just a few kilometers away, in Sonceboz-Sombeval. In 2001, the two companies were united to form Weber + Calibra SA in Sonceboz-Sombeval. In 2006, Hugo's son Pierre Weber, certified mechanical engineer and EPFL physics engineer, took the lead of the company.

The tradition continues

Weber + Calibra SA has a capital of 600 000 CHF. The company currently employs 15 collaborators, and produces 500 tonnes per year of precision alloys for the watchmaking industry and other specialties for precision markets.
