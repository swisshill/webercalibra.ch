Some technical specifications

Calibrated strips that are 0.5 mm - 1.5 mm thick with a tolerance of +/- 0.3%. Maximum width of 48 mm.

Dimensions of alloys: 0.05 mm - 3.00 mm thick and 3.00 mm - 135.00 mm wide.

Our width and thickness tolerances are tighter than DIN norms. Tighter norms are possible upon request.

The degree of alloy hardness may be expressed in terms of Vickers values (HV). The terms extra-soft, hard and extra-hard are also used.

Surface conditions are suitable for watchmaking requirements.

We deliver

Rolls of coil diameters between 100 m – 600 mm, depending on thickness and manufacturing possibilities. For large rolls, the maximum outer diameter is 1000 mm.

Fixed lengths of choice between 0.5m – 4m, with a maximum width of 135 mm.

Disk diameters of choice between 14 mm – 60 mm

Stamped pieces, forced and normal, for the watchmaking industry. Stamps must be provided by the client.
